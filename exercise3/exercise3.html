Behold, the Penguin King. Born in the raging winter of 2020, one of the fiercest nights ever recorded, December 18th, 2020. This monster is a ruthless leader who makes no exception for any penguin. Subjected to the coldest parts of Antartica, the Penguin King faced many challenges through its youth. Despite the rigorous upcoming, the Penguin King came to rule every inch of the Antartican land.
